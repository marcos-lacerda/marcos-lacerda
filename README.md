## 🧭 About Me

I am a **Software Engineer** with experience in **full-stack** and **mobile development**, and strong expertise in **software architecture**.  
Currently, I’m focused on building **Agent Trip**, a travel-organization app that I designed and have been developing independently — managing all stages from **design and development** to **infrastructure and product strategy**.

Over **8 years at Turbi**, I was part of the company from day zero to its consolidation as a national reference in **digital car rental**.  
I led the development of the first iOS app, supported scaling from **0 to 5,300 cars**, surpassed **1 million bookings**, and helped the company reach **R$271M in revenue in 2024**, with a **4.8 App Store rating** based on **28,000+ reviews**.

I hold a **Bachelor’s degree in Systems Analysis and Development** and an **MBA in Digital Business**, combining technical expertise with a strategic vision focused on innovation and technology.

Recently, I have attended key tech events such as **Apple Intelligence & Machine Learning (Apple São Paulo)**, **AI Day (StartSe)**, and **MongoDB Local São Paulo**, staying up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements.
